Airkit Hack

SCIENCE: IT'S WHAT'S FOR DINNER!

When?

Thursday, Friday. March 17 + 18, 2022

Who?

Everyone! All Team's everywhere within Airkit

Where?

Wherever you are!

What?

A two day major hacking event. Find teammates to work on a project and address an area where you think the company can grow. Points given for collaboration, audacity, heroic attempts, cross discipline work, and try hard.

At the end of the two days your team will present to the company in 3 minutes or less what you accomplished and why you think the company should do this.

Why?

Airkit is committed to innovation. One way we show this by asking ourselves what would make things better. We take the time, once a quarter, to allow teammates to show ideas they feel passionate about.
